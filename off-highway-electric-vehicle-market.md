<p><p>The off-highway electric vehicle market comprises primarily two types: Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) and Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs). BEVs operate solely on electric power stored in batteries, offering zero emissions and lower operating costs, making them ideal for applications like agriculture and construction. HEVs combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor, providing extended range and improved fuel efficiency, making them suitable for more demanding tasks where full electric may not suffice. Both types contribute to sustainability efforts in various industries.</p></p>
