<p><p>The Polyoxymethylene (POM) plastic market is divided into two main types: Homo-polyoxymethylene and Co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polyoxymethylene features a high degree of polymerization and offers superior mechanical properties, making it ideal for precision parts and applications requiring high strength and rigidity. Co-polyoxymethylene, on the other hand, incorporates different monomers, enhancing flexibility and chemical resistance. This versatility allows for broader applications in industries such as automotive, electronics, and consumer goods, catering to diverse performance requirements and processing techniques.</p></p>
