<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Fan</li><li>Axial Fan</li><li>Mixed Flow Fan</li><li>Cross Flow Fan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic compact fan market includes various types designed for specific airflow requirements. Centrifugal fans generate high pressure and are ideal for HVAC systems. Axial fans move air along the axis and are commonly used in cooling applications. Mixed flow fans combine features of both, offering a balance between pressure and flow. Cross flow fans provide a uniform airflow across a surface, often utilized in appliances like heaters and air conditioners. Each type serves unique industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
