<p><strong>The Power Resistors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chassis Mounted</li><li>Surface Mounted</li><li>Through-Hole Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power resistors market can be categorized into three main types: chassis mounted, surface mounted, and through-hole mounted. Chassis mounted resistors are designed for robust applications, often enclosed in a metal casing, providing excellent thermal management. Surface mounted resistors are compact and ideal for high-density circuit designs, allowing for automated assembly. Through-hole mounted resistors offer strong mechanical support and are commonly used in traditional PCB designs, providing easier repair and modification options. Each type serves distinct applications based on size, mounting technique, and thermal performance.</p></p>
