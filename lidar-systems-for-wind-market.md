<p><p>Lidar systems are essential for assessing wind resources and optimizing wind energy production. Ground-mounted Lidar systems are deployed at various locations to measure wind speed and direction at different heights, providing valuable site-specific data. In contrast, nacelle-mounted Lidar systems are installed on the turbine itself, enabling real-time monitoring of wind conditions directly affecting turbine performance. Both systems enhance efficiency in wind farm operations, aiding in better placement of turbines and improving energy yield predictions.</p></p>
