<p><p>The influenza diagnostic equipment market includes various types of tests to identify influenza viruses. Rapid Influenza Diagnostic Tests (RIDTs) offer quick results and are easy to use, providing immediate detection in clinical settings. Reverse Transcription Polymerase Chain Reaction (RT-PCR) is a more sensitive technique that detects viral RNA, making it ideal for confirming cases. Cell culture involves growing the virus in a laboratory environment, allowing for detailed analysis but requiring more time. Other methods may include serological tests and various novel technologies.</p></p>
