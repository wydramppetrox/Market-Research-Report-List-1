Parallel Plate PECVD Systems utilize parallel electrodes to create a uniform plasma over substrates, ideal for large area coatings. Tube PECVD Systems feature a tubular design, optimizing gas flow and plasma generation, which is suitable for smaller components and specialized applications, offering versatility in production processes.</p></p>
