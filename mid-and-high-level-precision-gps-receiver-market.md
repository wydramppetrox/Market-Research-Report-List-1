<p><p>The mid-and high-level precision GPS receiver market comprises two main types: single frequency and dual frequency receivers. Single frequency receivers operate on one frequency, typically offering basic positioning capabilities suitable for general applications. In contrast, dual frequency receivers utilize two frequencies, enhancing accuracy and reducing errors caused by atmospheric conditions. This makes dual frequency units ideal for professional applications requiring higher precision, such as surveying and aviation, thereby catering to diverse user needs across various industries.</p></p>
