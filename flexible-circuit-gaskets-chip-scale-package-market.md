<p><p>The Flexible Circuit Gaskets Chip Scale Package market comprises two primary types: Tab Upside Down and Inner Wire Bonding. Tab Upside Down involves flipping the chip to position its pads facing the substrate, enhancing thermal performance and making efficient use of space. Inner Wire Bonding, on the other hand, allows for connections between the chip and packaging substrate through wires, facilitating electrical connectivity while optimizing design. Both types enable advanced packaging solutions for high-density applications in electronics, improving reliability and performance.</p></p>
