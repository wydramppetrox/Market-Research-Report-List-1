<p><ul><li>Solid Tires</li><li>Pneumatic Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kick scooter tire market primarily features two types: solid tires and pneumatic tires. Solid tires are made from a durable rubber compound, offering low maintenance and puncture resistance, making them ideal for urban commutes. They provide a smoother ride on well-paved surfaces. Pneumatic tires, on the other hand, are air-filled, allowing for better shock absorption and enhanced grip on uneven terrain. They offer a more comfortable riding experience but require more maintenance due to potential punctures.</p></p>
