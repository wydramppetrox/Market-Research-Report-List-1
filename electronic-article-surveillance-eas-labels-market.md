<p><strong>The Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) Labels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acousto-Magnetic (AM) EAS</li><li>Microwave EAS</li><li>Electro-Magnetic EAS</li><li>UHF, Gen 2 RFID EAS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) labels encompass various technologies to prevent theft in retail. Acousto-Magnetic (AM) uses magnetic fields for detection, offering robust security. Microwave EAS employs microwave signals for tag detection, suitable for diverse environments. Electro-Magnetic EAS uses low-frequency electromagnetic fields, ideal for library security. UHF technology excels in long-range detection and provides real-time inventory management. Gen 2 RFID EAS combines RFID with security features, allowing for efficient tracking and enhanced loss prevention strategies in retail settings.</p></p>
