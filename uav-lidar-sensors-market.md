<p><p>The UAV LiDAR sensors market is primarily categorized into solid-state and mechanical LiDAR sensors. Solid-state LiDAR sensors utilize fixed components with no moving parts, resulting in enhanced durability, reduced weight, and lower maintenance requirements. They offer higher reliability for various applications, including forestry and agriculture. In contrast, mechanical LiDAR sensors feature rotating components, providing a wider field of view and detailed 3D mapping capabilities. Despite being bulkier and more prone to wear, they excel in high-resolution data collection over extensive areas.</p></p>
