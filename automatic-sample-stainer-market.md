<p><ul><li>Continuous Feed Stainer</li><li>Batch Stainer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Sample Stainer market includes two primary types: continuous feed stainers and batch stainers. Continuous feed stainers are designed for high-throughput laboratories, allowing for the uninterrupted processing of samples, which increases efficiency and reduces turnaround time. In contrast, batch stainers operate by processing discrete groups of samples in cyclical batches, making them suitable for smaller facilities or specialized applications. Both types enhance staining consistency and accuracy, catering to varying laboratory needs in histology and cytology.</p></p>
