<p><p>Harmonic gear drives come in three main types: Cup Style, Hat Style, and Pancake Style. Cup Style features a deep cup-shaped flexible component, ideal for high torque and compact designs. Hat Style resembles an inverted hat, providing a larger surface area for load distribution and greater stiffness, suitable for applications needing precision and reliability. Pancake Style is flat and compact, making it ideal for low-profile applications where space is limited while still delivering efficient torque transmission and high reduction ratios.</p></p>
